Attachment : Many resources in the application have attached file(s) with them. This model has/implements? functions to find the related resource.

College : The office of the department of computer science overlooks many a procedures related to computer science examinations conducted at the UG level colleges affiliated to DU. Each college has information related to it, more importantly the programmes it offers in computer science.

Programmes : Each college affiliated to DU offers a different list of programmes in computer science. Examples of programmes being BSc(H) CS, BSc(programme) CS etc. Each programme has a different list of courses
